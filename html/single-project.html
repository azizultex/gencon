Health Care Network Corporate Office HQ

Type of Project Goes Here
Location of Project Would Go Here

Interior fit-out of an office building in an existing core & shell space. Fit-out included creation of new offices, conference rooms and support areas on two separate levels, new carpet, paint, and ceilings throughout, new HVAC and lighting systems including custom lighting controls, plumbing for added sinks in Staff Areas (kitchenettes), a Reception area with custom reception desk and floating soffit above, all-glass entry doors, and an over-sized executive boardroom on the 2nd floor. An outdoor roof deck with custom trellis and alternative wood decking will be built in the spring as part of this project’s scope of work.
